# Healthcare Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes hospital transaction data to uncover insights into revenue performance, profitability, and patient trends across different medical specialties.

The goal is to support data-driven decision-making in healthcare operations.

---

## Objectives
- Track hospital revenue and profit performance
- Analyze patient and doctor distribution
- Identify high-performing and underperforming specialties
- Detect cost inefficiencies in medical procedures

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI (Data Modeling, DAX, Visualization)
- Excel (Data Cleaning)

---

## Key Insights
- Dermatology is the top-performing specialty, generating the highest revenue (~65K) and profit (~19.4K)
- Cardiology and Ophthalmology also contribute significantly to overall revenue
- Orthopedics has the lowest profit (~9.7K), indicating possible cost inefficiencies
- Revenue peaked in 2022 Q1, suggesting strong seasonal or operational performance
- Certain procedures (e.g., MRI scans) have high costs relative to revenue, reducing profitability
- Patient volume remains relatively stable, but revenue fluctuates, indicating pricing or service mix variations

---

## Business Recommendations
- Increase investment in high-performing specialties such as Dermatology and Cardiology
- Investigate and optimize cost structures in Orthopedics
- Adjust pricing strategies for low-margin procedures
- Leverage high-performing periods (e.g., Q1) for strategic expansion and marketing

---

## Data Model
The dataset follows a **star schema model**:

- Fact Table: Transactions
- Dimension Tables: Patients, Doctors, Procedures


## Key Features
- Interactive KPI tracking
- Revenue trend analysis (Year & Quarter)
- Profitability breakdown by specialty
- Patient and doctor performance insights
- Dynamic filtering (Year, Gender, State)
